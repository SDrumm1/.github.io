About

PMO and Project Controls professional with a background that combines hands-on programme delivery, commercial leadership and over a decade of operating in regulated and compliance driven environments.

I have supported complex project delivery by coordinating stakeholders across technical and operational teams, managing requirements and dependencies, producing governance reporting, and contributing to delivery assurance on programmes up to £10m in value — including a recent ERP implementation where my involvement contributed to a 40% improvement in inventory accuracy and £15k+ in annual cost savings.

That commercial background gives me a different perspective on what good delivery actually looks like.

I am currently completing the APM Project Fundamentals Qualification (PFQ), with a clear focus on PMO Analyst and Project Controls roles within nuclear and critical infrastructure — sectors where rigorous governance and delivery assurance are foundational.

Based in Warrington. Open to hybrid roles across the North West.
